About us

Welcome to Thread and Rope!

Every thread tells a story. I pour my heart into each knot, carefully crafting macrame designs that reflect my creativity and dedication to quality. I am passionate about intertwining art and craftsmanship to create macrame pieces that transform your home with beauty and elegance.

My macrame creations encompass a wide range of styles, from intricate wall hangings that add a touch of bohemian charm to your home, to plant hangers that bring nature indoors, creating a serene and cozy atmosphere. Your living spaces will feel warmer, inviting, and full of character.

With a deep appreciation for the art of macrame, I also offer custom designs tailored to your preferences. Whether you have a specific vision in mind or need guidance in selecting the perfect macrame piece for your space, I will provide a personalized experience, ensuring that your unique style shines through in each thread and knot.

Thread and Rope is committed to sustainability and ethical practices, working with environmentally friendly materials.

We invite you to explore our collection and discover the artistry of macrame. Whether you're looking to adorn your home or find the perfect gift for a loved one, Thread and Rope is here to help you create spaces filled with style and a touch of macrame magic.

Join me on this creative journey and experience the creations of Thread and Rope!
